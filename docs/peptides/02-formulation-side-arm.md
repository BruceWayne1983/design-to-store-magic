## Naming system

**`PEPTI-[ROLE] [CODE][SEQUENCE]`**

| Role tag | Meaning |
|---|---|
| BUILD | muscle / MPS |
| RECON | joint, tendon, recovery |
| GLO | skin / female-focused / beauty |
| FLOW | vascular / blood pressure |
| LEAN | satiety / natural GLP-1 / metabolic |
| CALM | sleep / stress / parasympathetic |

The code letter signals the active class (M = muscle, J = joint, S = skin, V = vascular, E = endocrine/metabolic, N = nervous-system). The number is an internal SKU sequence — same pattern as Infinis "L5", "B10", "M85" — so the brand reads the same in search results without copying their codes.

Every SKU keeps **PEPTI-** in the URL slug, H1, page title, alt text, supplement-fact panel — i.e. peptide-keyword density mirrors what Infinis has built but expressed in Baseline's clinical voice.

## SKU 1 — PEPTI-BUILD M14

**Tagline:** Muscle protein synthesis, accelerated.
**Target user:** Lifters and physique athletes wanting maximal MPS per scoop. Anyone over 35 dealing with anabolic resistance.
**Format:** 1,050g powder · 30 servings · 35g scoop. Two flavours at launch (Vanilla, Chocolate).
**Price:** £54.99 (1 tub), Subscribe & Save £49.99.

### Key ingredients (per 35g serving)

| Ingredient | Dose | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Lacprodan® BLG-100 (β-lactoglobulin isolate) | 15,000 mg | High natural leucine yield — peptide-rich base |
| Whey Protein Hydrolysate (DH ≥18%) | 12,000 mg | Di- and tripeptides, fast Tmax |
| PepForm® Leucine Peptides | 3,000 mg | Targeted leucine delivery via peptide carrier |
| Creatine Monohydrate (Pürest grade) | 3,000 mg | Cross-promo with existing Pürest Creatine |
| AstraGin® | 50 mg | Compounding amino acid uptake — already in Baseline range |
| MAX Catalyst™ | 5 mg | Absorption amplifier — Baseline house ingredient |

**Total protein per serving:** 27g · **Total leucine:** 5g+ · **No added carbs/fillers/lecithin**.

### 3-step mechanism

| Step | What happens |
|---|---|
| **01 — Peptide absorption** | Hydrolysate di-/tripeptides clear gastric phase faster than intact whey. Plasma amino acid Cmax hits within 30–45 minutes. |
| **02 — Leucine spike to mTORC1** | 5g+ leucine — well above the 3g leucine threshold for maximal MPS activation. PepForm® Leucine Peptides drive a steeper, sharper spike. |
| **03 — Sustained anabolic window** | β-lactoglobulin matrix and creatine pair MPS activation with ATP-resynthesis substrate for the next session. AstraGin® compounds amino-acid transporter activity at 4 weeks. |

**Suggested use:** 1 scoop (35g) in 300–400ml cold water or milk, within 30 minutes post-training. On rest days, take any time.

**Differentiators vs Infinis Protein Peptide L5:**
1. **+3g creatine built in** — Infinis charges separately. Baseline cross-promotes Pürest.
2. **AstraGin® + MAX Catalyst™** — proprietary Baseline absorption stack Infinis doesn't carry.
3. **£54.99 vs $57.99** — under-price the equivalent in GBP, premium positioning preserved.

**Position vs existing range:** Does not cannibalise. Fusion Lite+, VASCUL8™, GLYCOSHIFT™ are pre/intra. PEPTI-BUILD M14 is the post-training piece Baseline has been missing. Cross-sells: Pürest Creatine (loading phase), Electro Flow (recovery).

**COGS estimate:** ~£10.50/tub (~19% of RRP). Hydrolysate and BLG-100 are the cost drivers.

| Step | What happens |

**Differentiators vs Infinis Flow Peptide B10:**

**Position vs existing range:** Slots between Performance and Health. Does not cannibalise; complements Pürest Creatine (creatine + collagen is a documented synergy for connective tissue under load). Cross-sells: Pürest Creatine, VASCUL8™.

**COGS estimate:** ~£9.00/tub (~21% of RRP). Tendoforte® and Fortigel® licensing is the cost driver.

## Launch order recommendation

| Wave | Window | SKUs | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Wave 1 — Lead** | Day 0 | **PEPTI-BUILD M14** & **PEPTI-RECON J22** | Direct strike on Infinis's flagship protein-peptide segment. M14 is the head-to-head competitive SKU; J22 captures connective-tissue search volume Infinis under-serves. Both convert against Baseline's existing performance audience. |
| **Wave 2 — Expand** | Day 90 | **PEPTI-GLO S30** & **PEPTI-LEAN E08** | Open the female-skewing and body-composition segments. GLO unlocks beauty/skin keywords. LEAN captures the surging "natural GLP-1" search volume (riding on semaglutide media coverage without regulatory risk). |

**Why not all six at once:** content/cluster build per SKU is ~6–8 weeks of editorial. Six simultaneous launches would dilute paid-media and PR per SKU. Two-at-a-time keeps spend concentrated and SEO content cluster builds compounding.
